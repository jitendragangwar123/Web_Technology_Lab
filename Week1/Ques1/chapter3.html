B.Tech
BTech stands for Bachelor of technology. It is an undergraduate engineering degree awarded to a candidate. The duration of this degree course is four years.
M.Tech
MTech Full-Form, Master of Technology. Course Duration, 2-Years. Admission Process, Entrance Exam Based.
PhD
PhD is short for Doctor of Philosophy. This is an academic or professional degree that, in most countries, qualifies the degree holder to teach their chosen subject at university level or to work in a specialized position in their chosen field.
